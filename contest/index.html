1. Eligibility

C-4A Centennial Conclave Contest (the Contest) is open to legal residents of the fifty (50) United States and the District of Columbia who are registered, active, dues-paid, members of lodges of the Order of the Arrow, Boy Scouts of America, which are organized under the Central Region, Area 4, Section A at any point throughout the duration of the contest, as determined by the Sponsor. Employees of the Sponsor, Order of the Arrow, Boy Scouts of America and other companies associated with the promotion of the Contest, and their respective parents, subsidiaries, affiliates and advertising and promotion agencies as well as the immediate family (spouse, parents, siblings, and children) and household members of each such employee are not eligible. The Contest is subject to federal, state, and local laws and regulations.

Entries that are incomplete, illegible, fraudulent, or corrupted are void and will not be accepted. If such an entry is awarded a prize in error, it may be revoked and issued to a valid entry at the discretion of the Sponsor without notice to the originator of the voided entry.

2. Sponsor

The Contest is sponsored by Section A, Central Region Area 4, Order of the Arrow, Boy Scouts of America, located at 6 Quail Ct, Wheeling, West Virginia 26003 (the Sponsor).

3. Agreement to Official Rules

Participation in the Contest constitutes entrants full and unconditional agreement to and acceptance of these Official Rules and the decisions of the Sponsor, which are final and binding. Winning a prize is contingent upon being compliant with these Official Rules and fulfilling all other requirements set forth herein.

4. Contest Period

The Contest begins on December 21, 2014 at 12:00 am Eastern Standard Time and ends on January 31, 2015 at 11:59 pm Eastern Standard Time (the Contest Period), as determined by the clock on the server of the Sponsor. Entries that are submitted before or after the Contest Period will be disqualified. Submissions will be accepted for the duration of the Contest using any of the following methods: Online.

5. How to Enter

Online: This method of entry will be available by visiting our website (http://conclave.oa-c4a.org/contest) and following the directions provided to fill out the entry information, and submit. Limit one (1) entry per person, per email address for the duration of the Contest Period, regardless of method of entry. Entries received from any person, e-mail address, or household in excess of the stated limitation will be void. All entries become the property of Sponsor and will not be acknowledged or returned.

6. Prize Drawing

The Grand Prize will be awarded to a youth entrant to whom a provisional award of another prize has not been made, as defined by the Sponsor. On or about February 01, 2015, the Sponsor will select the potential winner in a random drawing from among all eligible entries received. The odds of being selected depend on the number of entries received. The Sponsor will attempt to notify the potential winner via telephone or email on or about February 02, 2015. If the potential winner cannot be contacted within five (5) days after the date of the first attempt to contact him/her, the Sponsor may select an alternate potential winner in his/her place at random from the remaining non-winning, eligible entries.

For all other prizes, potential winners will be notified immediately following their entry on the Contest web site. The odds of winning any given prize are outlined in the Prizes section of these rules. All such prizes will be awared on a provisional basis, while supplies last, pending the validation of the winner's entry at the end of the contest period. Should any of these prizes remain unpromised at the end of the contest period, the Sponsor will award them under the same provisional terms to entrants selected at random from all valid entries, provided they have not already been provisionally awarded another prize.

7. Winner Notification

The potential winner(s) will be notified by email only. The potential Grand Prize winner (parent/legal guardian if a minor in his/her state of residence) will be required to complete, electronically sign and submit a Declaration of Compliance within five (5) days of the date notice or attempted notice is sent, in order to claim his/her prize. If a potential winner cannot be contacted, or fails to submit the Declaration of Compliance within the required time period (if applicable), or prize is returned as undeliverable, potential winner forfeits prize. If the potential winner is at least 18 but still considered a minor in his/her jurisdiction of residence, Sponsor reserves the right to award the prize in the name of his/her parent or legal guardian, who will be required to sign the Declaration of Compliance on the potential winners behalf and fulfill any other requirements imposed on winner set forth herein.

Potential winners of other prizes will be notified by email once their entries have been validated and provided a unique link by which to claim their prize. All potential winners must complete the procedure to claim their prize by April 01, 2015 or the prize will be considered forefeited.

Potential winners must continue to comply with all terms and conditions of these Official Rules, and winning is contingent upon fulfilling all requirements. In the event that a potential winner is disqualified for any reason or their prize is forefeited, Sponsor may award, at the Sponsor's discretion, the applicable prize to an alternate winner by random drawing from among all remaining eligible entries. If the Sponsor so chooses to maximum of one (1) alternate drawings will be held, with one week allowed following the drawing for the new potential winner to complete the claim process, after which the prize will remain un-awarded. Prizes successfully claimed will be fulfilled upon the winner's appearance at check-in at the Conclave 2015 event.

8. Prizes

One (1) Grand Prize voucher for free registration to Conclave 2015 ($50 value). Odds of winning depend on the number of entries.

Two (2) vouchers for a commemorative t-shirt (approximately $15 value). Odds of winning: 1 in 750

Twelve (12) invitations to VIA luncheon (no retail value). Odds of winning: 1 in 125

One hundred (100) vouchers for a free collectible patch (approximately $5 value). Odds of winning: 1 in 20
